Background: Luke owns a luxurious beachfront property in Miami that offers breathtaking ocean views and top-tier amenities. Despite slashing prices and offering discounts, Luke found it difficult to attract guests. His conventional marketing efforts fell flat, leaving him increasingly anxious about his property’s sustainability.

Luke’s Perspective: "My property is my pride and joy, but every year the off-season hit me hard. I tried everything—price cuts, promotions, you name it—but nothing stuck.
